About us

Helping Hands is a community service to help members of our society.

What we do?

We record your complaint and forward it to the concerned authorities for actions. We provide the real-time status of the actions on your complaints, that are available to the public to observe. And provide statical data based on location and type of complaints submitted on the platform.

Our Objective

To make our society a happy place by helping every individual in every way possible.
